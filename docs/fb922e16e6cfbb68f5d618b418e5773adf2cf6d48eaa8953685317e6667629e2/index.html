About the data

This register has reported a total of 33 brownfield sites.

9 of these have an end date and are no longer considered brownfield land.

Excluding sites with an end date, there are 26.23 hectares of land.

This land has the potential to provide between 1067 and 1098 dwellings.

0 of these sites have permission in principle.
